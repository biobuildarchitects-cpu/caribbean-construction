Project 1 – American-Inspired Residence with Mediterranean Accents

A detailed featured presentation for your first actual project. The images below use your folder filenames, so they will display once those image files are placed in the same folder as the website file.

A single-storey residential project designed with an American-Inspired Residence with Mediterranean Accents approach, emphasizing comfort, functionality, and clean architectural lines.

The house features a well-balanced layout with generous interior spaces, a covered carport, and seamless indoor-outdoor connection suited for the Batangas climate. The design incorporates wide roof overhangs for sun and rain protection, along with proper ventilation to enhance thermal comfort.

The exterior is defined by a combination of light-colored finishes, textured elements, and a low-profile roof, creating a refined yet welcoming residential character. The landscaped front yard complements the structure, enhancing both curb appeal and overall livability.

This project reflects Caribbean Construction’s commitment to delivering well-built, practical, and aesthetically pleasing homes tailored to the client’s needs and environment.

Project Highlights

Project Type: Single-Storey Residential House

Design Style: American-Inspired Residence with Mediterranean Accents
